About Crisees

Crisees is a proof-of-concept prototype application which monitors and analyses social media streams in near real-time, providing information which may be of great use to governmental officials about a particular event.

This web front-end makes use of the Crisees API, an open framework allowing for access to the information available via - and modification of - the Crisees framework.

The prototype forms part of the fourth year Computing Science project of David Maxwell at the University of Glasgow, for academic year 2011-2012.
